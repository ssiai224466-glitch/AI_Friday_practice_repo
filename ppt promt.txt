Title: Infrastructure Capacity Planning Advisor Using Generative AI

Problem Statement:

Planning IT infrastructure capacity involves analyzing usage trends, growth forecasts, and application demands, which is complex and error-prone. Suboptimal planning leads to resource wastage or performance degradation. A generative AI agent that synthesizes historical data and business growth inputs to recommend capacity plans can improve accuracy and agility. This supports better budgeting and architecture decisions.

Data Requirements:

Historical infrastructure usage metrics (CPU, memory, storage)

Business growth projections and application deployment plans

Cost models and vendor constraints

Configuration details of current infrastructure components

(Feel free to generate synthetic data as well from the provided access)

Expected Output:

The output consists of textual capacity planning reports with resource forecasts, risk assessments, and budgeting recommendations. Reports are formatted for IT management review and should support exporting as PDF or Excel files.
